Every course on Know is taught by a trained expert from a top university or company. You may access on-demand video lectures, homework tasks, and community discussion forums. Paid courses include more tests and assignments, as well as a shareable Course Certificate at the end.
Who we are.... We are a website which aims to give to Pakistan what has never been readily available here; online education. Hundreds of free courses give you access to on-demand video lectures, homework exercises, and community discussion forums. Certifications are available for paid courses upon completion, and may even be added to degree credits. Furthermore,
we also offer skill-based crash courses to learn skills that are high in-demand and may allow you to secure a job with no degree.
With the rising popularity in MOOCs worldwide,
and the troubles face by Pakistani students in high-school
and university, we envisioned a website that would allow everyone to have the best learning experiences.

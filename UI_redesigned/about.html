About Us

We are a website who aim to give to Pakistan what has never been readily available here; online education. Hundreds of free courses give you access to on-demand video lectures, homework exercises, and community discussion forums. Certifications are available for paid courses upon completion, and may even be added to degree credits. Furthermore,
we also offer skill-based crash courses to learn skills that are high in-demand and may allow you to secure a job with no degree.

Courses

Every course on Know is taught by a certified professional from world-class universities and companies, so you can learn something new anytime, anywhere. Hundreds of free courses give you access to on-demand video lectures, homework exercises, and community discussion forums. Paid courses provide additional quizzes and projects as well as a shareable Course Certificate upon completion.

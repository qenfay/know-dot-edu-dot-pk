General Pricing

Our pricing is done keeping in mind the need and financial ability of all potential students and professionals looking to avail the aid provided by our website. As the internet has become incredibly widespread, with even the less fortunate often having internet at home and data in their phones, our prices must reflect that. As such, as a general rule, we offer up to a 50% discount on courses according to financial needs. Courses that are already complete (not live), are available later as resources at a 25% reduced price. Free courses are also offered. However, they are not certified, do not include quizzes and assessments and cannot count as credits either.
